Sean Hinkson

I am Sean Hinkson and I am a designer at Eastern Washington University in my senior year. I enjoy the design program at Eastern Washinton University and I am excited to take my knowladge beyond the schools program. Many of the works you will see in this Portfolio are things that I have worked on in school and revised for this project.

Project 1

For this project our task was to create a book cover for the book/movie of our choice. I chose the title "Atlantis, The Lost Empire" a classic Disney movie that I know and love from watching many hours of it. We used figure ground to display important Characters and locations from the film.

Project 2

For this project I was challenged to take detailed architectural shots of buildings in the surrounding area. A church in downtown Spokane made easy work of this as the sculptures and detail in the buildings made for some amazing pictures and photo editing.

Project 3

For this project I created a made up company and product they would sell to advertise for it. I chose "Not So Normal" as my company name and decided skate wheels to be the main focus of the product packaging. We mocked up our ideas and built them from scratch.
